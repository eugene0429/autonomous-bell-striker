Phase 2 Aiming — Center-Aim with Predictive Firing

After Phase 1, the camera sits exactly under the bell — but the launcher is mounted on a leveling platform offset from the camera on the rover. The bell follows a triangle-wave motion (constant velocity within each half-period, random speed re-sampled at every endpoint). The system (1) calibrates Y_min / Y_max of the bell trajectory for 10 s, (2) aims the launcher at the trajectory midpoint Y_mid, (3) tracks the bell, (4) on LOAD, computes ball flight time τ and waits until firing τ seconds before the bell crosses Y_mid, then fires.
